Hub Asia

Singapore is one of the world's leading integrated logistics hub with knowledge-intensive supply chain management skills and technologies. As a global distribution centre, Hub Asia fits well in this strategic location standing on an area of 14,000 square meters space at Penjuru Lane. Hub Asia was incorporated in 1996 as the South East Asia regional distribution centre for 5,500 Schneider Electric product references. By the year 2011, it will handle over 15,000 product references to serve the world. Hub Asia in Singapore complements the company's hub in Europe, which geographically helps Schneider Electric achieve faster time to market, which is critical for its customers in the region.
